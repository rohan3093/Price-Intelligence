  • Stack CTAs full-width instead of side-by-side
  • Consider sticky Buy/Sell bar at bottom of screen

  • Unique value prop - Competitive advantage; no other platform does this
  • Trader-friendly - Appeals to power users treating sneakers as investments
  • Adjustable thresholds - Users set personal ROI/profit minimums
  • Liquidity column - Shows buy/sell depth to assess execution feasibility
  • Clear metrics - Both absolute (₹) and relative (%) profit shown
  • Info tooltip - Explains arbitrage concept for novices
  • Visual hierarchy - High ROI opportunities highlighted with green background

⚠ Issues & Concerns

  • Niche audience - Most users want to buy OR sell, not arbitrage
  • Execution complexity hidden - Implies easy profit but doesn't mention capital, timing, risk
  • Default filters too loose - 3% ROI shows dozens of marginal opportunities
  • Mobile table issues - 7 columns challenging on mobile (same issue as Listings)
  • No "Execute" workflow - Shows opportunity but no guidance on how to actually do it
  • Liquidity unclear - "Buy: 2, Sell: 5" - is that enough? No guidance on minimums
  • Fees unaccounted - Profit calculation may not include platform fees, shipping costs

→ Recommendations

  • Critical Default to CLOSED - Advanced feature; don't distract casual users
  • Critical Add execution workflow: "Execute Arb" button → step-by-step modal guide
  • High Raise default ROI to 5%: Filter out noise, show compelling opportunities only
  • High Add risk disclaimer tooltip: "Arbitrage requires capital, timing, carries risk"
  • Medium Mobile: Card view showing Buy → Sell → Profit flow vertically
  • Medium Add "Difficulty" indicator: Easy/Medium/Hard based on liquidity + channel complexity
  • Medium Calculate actual fees: Show net profit after all costs (shipping, platform fees, etc.)
  • Low Alert system: "Notify me when ROI > 10%" for automated monitoring

🎯 Execution Workflow Design

  • Step 1: "Buy from WhatsApp (₹12,500)" - Direct link to seller contact
  • Step 2: "Wait for delivery (2-3 days)" - Progress tracker
  • Step 3: "List on Marketplace (₹14,200)" - Pre-filled form
  • Step 4: "Realize profit (est. ₹1,700)" - Net calculator after fees

High Value: Takes complex data and gives simple, actionable recommendation. Users love this.
Trust Building Needed: Should show track record: "Our insights are 78% accurate (based on 120 predictions)"

✓ Strengths

  • Clear recommendation - Distills complexity into BUY/SELL/HOLD action
  • Confidence % shown - Transparency: users know how certain AI is
  • Reasoning provided - Not black box; explains "why"
  • Visual prominence - Large colored border (green/red) + bold text unmissable
  • Open by default - High-value content surfaced automatically
  • Size-specific - Recommendation tied to selected size (9.5 in this case)
  • Expected movement - Forward-looking: "may rise 5-10%"

⚠ Issues & Concerns

  • Trust building needed - Users may not trust AI without historical track record
  • Basis unclear - What data drives this? (30d trend? Arbitrage? Supply?)
  • No CTA link - Says "BUY" but doesn't link to Buy modal/listings
  • Expected movement vague - "+5-10%" over what timeframe? 30 days? 90 days?
  • Size-specific warning missing - If for size 9.5, does it apply to size 10?
  • No alternative scenarios - What if price drops? When to reconsider?

→ Recommendations

  • Critical Add CTA button: If "BUY", show "Shop Now →" linking to hero Buy modal
  • Critical Show track record: "Our insights are 78% accurate (120 predictions tracked)"
  • High Clarify timeframe: "Expected: +5-10% over next 30 days"
  • High Add "Why?" explainer: Expandable "Based on: 30d trend (+8%), low supply (12 listings), seasonal demand"
  • Medium Confidence gauge: Visual meter (thermometer style) instead of just %
  • Medium Alternative scenarios: "If price drops below ₹11,500, consider waiting"
  • Low Community sentiment: "85% of users watching this asset (↑ from last week)"
  • Low Analyst notes: Optional human commentary overlay

💡 Enhanced Layout Idea

  • Top: BUY (85% confident) | [Shop Now →] button
  • Middle: Reasoning paragraph (current)
  • Bottom: Expandable "Show calculation basis" → bullet points of factors
  • Footer: "Track record: 78% accurate over 120 predictions"
